Under the hood

The Green Shield tech stack, built for impact.

Three core components work together to turn your everyday choices into measurable carbon impact — without compromising your privacy.

Engine
The Matchmaker
Not just “cheapest.” We calculate Carbon ROI — how much CO₂ you avoid per dollar when you switch. See the impact of moving your energy, super or loans before you commit.
Security
The Privacy Vault
Your identity is encrypted and separated from your usage data. We use Zero Trust Infrastructure and CDR‑based access so your data powers insights, not exploitation.
Intelligence

An independent scoring engine that ranks providers on emissions, performance and transparency. No commissions, no pay‑to‑play — just objective rankings.
